FAAN Key Brand Identity Components

Visual Identity: The rebranding focuses on a consistent, modern, and "globally attractive yet locally rooted" look across all airports.

Color Palette : A prominent, deep "Jewel" green (#157347) is used.

Logo: The FAAN logo has been updated and should appear consistently across all platforms, including digital, print, and physical, to project a "leader in airport business" image.

Signage & Aesthetics: Reimagining includes a completely redesigned commercial area with consistent signage, modern storefronts, and a passenger-centered design.

Tone: The brand focuses on safety, efficiency, and being "people-focused". 

Key Guidelines & Initiatives

Community-Centric Approach: The brand is shifting toward putting passenger experience and local culture at the forefront of its design and services.

Integrated Policy: Brand messaging is tied to high-quality standards (ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015).

Application: New branding is applied across all touchpoints, including uniforms, stationery, digital apps, website, and physical airport infrastructure.

Visibility: Enhanced branding is featured during major events like the FAAN National Aviation Conference (FNAC).
